Creativerse Sprites Picker

This tool lets you pick up sprite codes to be put into Arc Signs in the game Creativerse, free to play on Steam.

Check out the CreatiSign Generator to import your images into Arc Signs and the CreatiCount Tool to get some help counting materials and their ingredients for your builds!

How to use this picker

Grid mode (default): click on any icon to add it to the grid, then you can drag the grid icons around or drag them out of the grid to remove them. The yellow textarea gets updated automatically and you only need to copy it over to an Arc Sign when you're done. Careful that changing some params will empty the grid!

Free mode: click on any icon to have its code appended to the yellow textarea, then click the "Add intro code" button if you want the icons to get aligned to blocks, then copy all the code and paste it into an Arc Sign.

In both modes you may need to adjust the <offset> and <zoffset> tags depending on your design.
